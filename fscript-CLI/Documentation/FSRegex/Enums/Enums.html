Constants
FSRegexCaseInsensitive
Matching is case insensitive. Equivalent to /i in Perl.
FSRegexDotAll
Dot metacharacter matches any character including newline. Equivalent to /s in Perl.
FSRegexExtended
Allow whitespace and comments in the pattern. Equivalent to /x in Perl.
FSRegexLazy
Makes greedy quantifiers lazy and lazy quantifiers greedy. No equivalent in Perl.
FSRegexMultiline
Caret and dollar anchors match at newline. Equivalent to /m in Perl.
Discussion

Options defined for -initWithPattern:options:. Two or more options can be combined with the bitwise OR operator.
